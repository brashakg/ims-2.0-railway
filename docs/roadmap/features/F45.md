## Intended behavior (full intent)

The walkout CRM is the flagship lost-sale recovery system for an optical retail store. Every customer who leaves without buying is logged in 30 seconds by the salesperson. The store then runs a disciplined 2 (optionally 3) round follow-up chain to recover the sale. The system drives three outcomes:

**1. Capture intent + context.** When a customer walks out, the salesperson logs who they were, what they wanted, why they left, which competitor they mentioned, and when they plan to buy. This feeds the Scorecard conversion denominator and gives management insight into lost revenue patterns.

**2. Policy-driven follow-up.** Based on the walkout reason, the system suggests a follow-up action automatically:
- BUDGET/PRICE → trigger a promo voucher via E6 if a reminder_rule with `voucher_template` exists for this reason
- BRAND/COLOR/NOT AVAILABLE → flag for "notify on restock" (a future E6 event-triggered rule)
- STAFF BEHAVIOUR → immediately escalate to STORE_MANAGER as a P1 task (not just FU-due)
- All others → FU1 scheduled; system surfaces on the "FU Due Today" call-list

**3. Two-stage (2-round minimum, 3-round maximum) follow-up chain.**
- FU1 scheduled by the logging salesperson, typically within 2-3 days
- FU2 scheduled if FU1 does not convert, typically 7-14 days out
- FU3 (optional) for high-value prospects
- Each round has mode (CALL/WHATSAPP/SMS/EMAIL/IN-PERSON), supervisor, notes, status
- Anti-fake-closure: a salesperson marking FU DONE triggers `PENDING_APPROVAL` — a manager must verify the follow-up actually happened before it counts

**4. Result pipeline.** Outcomes: CONVERTED (links to an order_id), NEGATIVE (lost), DUE (still active). When CONVERTED, credit is split 50/50 between the logging salesperson and the closing salesperson (from the order).

**5. "FU Due Today" call-list.** Every morning the dashboard shows each salesperson's pending FUs due today. For WHATSAPP/SMS mode FUs, the outbound message passes through E6's gate stack (consent → quiet hours → 30-day/3-message cap). For CALL/IN-PERSON mode FUs, a Task is created instead of an outbound message. This is the "drive recovery without spamming" design.

**6. POS soft-block compliance banner.** When a salesperson opens a POS session or begins an order, if there are open walkouts from the last 48 hours for that store/salesperson, a dismissable compliance banner appears: "You have N walkouts without follow-up. Log them to maintain your conversion score." This NEVER blocks the sale.

**7. Conversion feed → Scorecard.** The `/conversion-feed` endpoint provides per-staff walk-ins, walkouts, retro-conversions, and a 0–20 conversion score that feeds the SC (Staff Scorecard) module. The 50/50 credit split means both the logging and closing associate get credit.

**8. Max 3 automated messages per customer per 30 days.** All outbound messages (WHATSAPP/SMS) from FU escalation pass through E6's frequency cap, never exceeding 3 promotional messages per customer per 30 days.
