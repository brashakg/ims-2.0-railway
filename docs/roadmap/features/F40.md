## Intended behavior (full intent)

ORACLE's nightly EOD sweep (22:00 IST) identifies every customer who is simultaneously a VIP (LTV >= 1,00,000 AND >= 3 completed orders) and overdue relative to their personal buying rhythm. "Overdue" is computed as: take all completed-order timestamps for the customer, compute the median gap between consecutive purchases, subtract last-purchase days-ago from that median. If a customer usually buys every 12 months and last bought 18 months ago, they are 6 months overdue — not caught by the flat 180-day global rule but genuinely at risk of loss.

ORACLE writes a `vip_churn_risk` subdoc back to each qualifying customer and appends a daily `vip_churn_snapshots` row (one per store per day). It calls Claude for the top 10 highest-scoring customers to produce a one-line narrative (fail-soft if `ANTHROPIC_API_KEY` absent).

SUPERADMIN and ADMIN (store-scoped) can view a dedicated watchlist at `/crm/vip-churn` ranked by overdue severity. When an admin decides to act, they click "Intervene" and pick a type: PERSONAL_CALL, EXCLUSIVE_OFFER, LOYALTY_BONUS, or WINBACK_WHATSAPP. This creates a P1 task in the `tasks` collection (via `create_system_task`, deduped per customer per 30 days) assigned to the store manager, writes an immutable `audit_logs` row, and — for WINBACK_WHATSAPP — fires MEGAPHONE's existing targeted send path, gated by `DISPATCH_MODE`.

The Customer 360 page for a VIP gains an upgraded churn-risk card: instead of "last purchased 200 days ago (HIGH)", it shows "Usual visit every 18 months — last visit 24 months ago — 6 months overdue". An inline "Intervene" shortcut calls the same endpoint without navigating away.

No order, price, or balance is touched anywhere in this feature. It is read + notification only.
